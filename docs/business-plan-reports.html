Municipal Project Control Platform · Version 4.0

4. Products & Services

4.1 Platform Overview

Itesho is a cloud-based project control platform designed specifically for municipalities to oversee construction projects, ensure compliance, and provide full accountability for public funds.

4.2 Core Modules

📊 Module 1: Dashboard

Real-time visibility into all municipal projects, budgets, approvals, and compliance status.

  • System operational status (Performance: 98%)
  • Active projects, budget tracking, pending approvals
  • Alerts: Compliance expirations, overdue approvals
  • Quick actions: Create project, generate invoice, approve submissions

📁 Module 2: Projects

Create and manage municipal construction projects with full lifecycle tracking.

  • Project details: budget, timeline, location, contractors
  • Assign contractors, sub-contractors, project team
  • View all projects with status indicators
  • Archive completed projects

🔒 Module 3: Compliance Gatekeeper

Enforce compliance before contractors can submit any work.

  • Compliance checklist: Safety file, insurance, permits, inductions, CIDB, COID
  • Document upload with expiry date tracking
  • Blocks access to daily logs if compliance incomplete
  • Full audit trail: Who approved what, when

📦 Module 4: RFQ Management

Create, distribute, and evaluate supplier quotations for municipal procurement.

  • Create RFQ with specifications and requirements
  • Distribute to multiple suppliers
  • Compare quotations side-by-side
  • Award contracts to selected supplier

📐 Module 5: Bill of Quantities (BOQ)

Track all work items, quantities, rates, and costs.

  • 3-level hierarchy: Section → Subsection → Line Items
  • Planned Quantity, Rate, Approved Quantity (only after PM approval)
  • Real-time progress tracking, budget tracking, cost breakdown
  • Export to Excel/PDF

📝 Module 6: Daily Logs

Contractors capture daily progress, resources, and photos.

  • Tabs: Work, Manpower, Equipment, Weather, Safety, Quality, Delays, Photos
  • Timestamp on submission, contractor name auto-captured
  • Save draft, submit for approval

✅ Module 7: Submissions (PM Approval)

Municipal PM reviews and approves contractor submissions before invoicing.

  • Table of all submitted daily logs: Date, Contractor, Work, Quantities, Status
  • Statuses: Pending, Approved, Rejected
  • One-click approval or rejection with comments
  • Full audit trail: Who approved what, when

💰 Module 8: Invoices

Auto-generated from PM-approved quantities. No manual calculations.

  • Approved Quantity × Rate = Invoice
  • Invoice line items: BOQ item, approved quantity, rate, total
  • Status: Draft, Submitted, Paid, Overdue
  • Export to PDF, payment tracking

📊 Module 9: Reports NEW

Real-time reporting for municipal accountability, SACAP compliance, and audit readiness.

The Reports module transforms project data into actionable insights for municipal decision-makers.

9.2 Business Impact

  • Accountability: Every action is tracked and reportable
  • Transparency: Municipalities can show exactly where funds are spent
  • SACAP Compliance: Generate required reports for National Treasury
  • Audit Readiness: Full audit trail for internal and external auditors
  • Informed Decisions: Real-time data for project and budget decisions

5. The Approval Chain

▐ ITESHO APPROVAL CHAIN ▐
STEP 1: COMPLIANCE GATEKEEPER
Project starts → Compliance checklist required
↓
Is compliance complete? → NO → Block access
→ YES → Proceed to Step 2
 
STEP 2: DAILY LOG SUBMISSION
Contractor submits daily log with quantities and photos
↓
Daily log submitted for municipal PM review
 
STEP 3: MUNICIPAL PM APPROVAL
PM reviews daily log against site reality and photos
↓
Is everything accurate? → NO → Reject
→ YES → Approve. Quantities lock in.
 
STEP 4: AUTO-GENERATED INVOICE
Approved quantities + Rate = Invoice
↓
Invoice ready for municipal payment
✅ PAYMENT (No disputes)
🔒 The Golden Rule: No compliance = No daily log = No municipal PM approval = No invoice.
