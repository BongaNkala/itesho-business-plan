Itesho — Complete Modules

Municipal Project Control Platform

📊 1. Dashboard

Single-screen visibility into all projects, budgets, approvals, and compliance status. System operational status with key metrics.

📁 2. Projects

Create and manage municipal construction projects with budget, timeline, location, and contractor assignments.

🔒 3. Compliance Gatekeeper

Verify contractor compliance (safety, insurance, CIDB, COID) before work can be submitted. Blocks non-compliant contractors.

📦 4. RFQ Management

Create, distribute, and evaluate supplier quotations for municipal procurement. Track responses and award contracts.

📐 5. Bill of Quantities (BOQ)

3-level hierarchy: Section → Subsection → Line Items. Track planned vs approved quantities, rates, and costs.

📝 6. Daily Logs

Contractors submit daily progress, quantities, manpower, equipment, photos. Timestamped and location-tracked.

✅ 7. Submissions (PM Approval)

Municipal PM reviews and approves contractor submissions. Full audit trail of who approved what, when.

💰 8. Invoices

Auto-generated from PM-approved quantities. Approved Qty × Rate = Invoice. No manual calculations.

📊 9. Reports NEW

Real-time reporting for municipal accountability and SACAP compliance.

  • Project Progress Report
  • Compliance Report
  • Approved Quantities Report
  • Invoice Summary Report
  • Budget vs Actual Report
  • Contractor Performance Report
  • Audit Trail Report
  • SACAP Compliance Report
